Instructions

Welcome to Nibsplosion! The knock-off version of Space Invaders- I mean a totally original game!
Nibbles (my favourite stuffed animal) embarked on a journey in space in his mission to conquer the world.
Unfortunately, snorlax - the world's guardian, cannot allow Nibbles to go against the balance or law of casuality
that has been holding the world together for years!
Help Nibbles dodge Snorlax's attacks and fight back!
(Unfortunately Nibbles is a lot weaker than Snorlax - him being a deity and all, so try holding back for as long as possible :) )


  • Press 'd' to move right
  • Press 'a' to move left
  • Press SPACE key to shoot
  • When Nibbles is hit, the game will end
  • Snorlax has a special skill, which allows him to make duplicates of itself at random times, and then dissapear like a mirage
